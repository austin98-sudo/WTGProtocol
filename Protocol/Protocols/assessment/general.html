General Information

  • Protocols in this section are designed to guide the EMS provider in the initial and ongoing approach to assessment and management of medical and trauma patients
  • The patient examination should focus on rapid assessment and interventions. On-scene management of high priority patients should be limited ot stabilization of life-threatening problems. Other procedures should always be performed while en-route to the hospital or a landing zone.
  • Scene time should be limited to rapid assessment and stabilization of life-threatening problems. Shorter scene tmies are desirable for trauma patients. Rescue efforts for patients that are entrapped or have access/egress problems should be coordinated to minimize scene time
  • The receiving hospital should be notified with an appropriate amount of time to prepare for the patient
  • At any time, if a provider is uncertain of how to best manage a patient, on-line [Medical Control] must be contacted for instruction
